FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using our platform

After placing an order, go to "My Orders" in your student dashboard. Click on any order to see detailed tracking information including current status, tracking number (once shipped), and estimated delivery date. You'll also receive email notifications for each status change.

We accept three payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery: Pay when you receive the product
  • bKash: Send payment to club's number, upload screenshot & transaction ID
  • Nagad: Send payment to club's number, upload screenshot & transaction ID

For mobile payments, clubs verify payments before shipping.

Delivery time depends on your location:

  • Dhaka City: 2-3 business days
  • Major Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other Areas: 5-7 business days

Clubs typically ship within 24 hours of order confirmation. You'll receive tracking details once shipped.

To become a verified club:

  1. Click "Sign Up" and select "University Club"
  2. Fill application with club details, university information
  3. Upload club logo and registration certificate
  4. Use official university email (.edu.bd domain)
  5. Submit and wait 24-48 hours for approval

Our team verifies documents and university affiliation. You'll receive email notification with approval status.

Commission rates vary by reward tier:

  • Bronze (0-499 points): 5%
  • Silver (500-1,499 points): 3%
  • Gold (1,500-4,999 points): 2%
  • Platinum (5,000+ points): 1%

Commission is automatically deducted from each sale. Higher tiers unlock lower rates and more benefits.

Clubs receive payouts on a weekly or monthly basis (based on preference):

  • View earnings in "My Earnings" dashboard
  • See breakdown by order with commission deduction
  • Minimum payout threshold: ৳1,000
  • Payments processed via bank transfer or bKash
  • Processing time: 2-3 business days

You'll receive email notification when payment is processed.

Clubs earn points through various activities:

  • +100 points: Organize a competition
  • +10 points: For every ৳100 in sales
  • +20 points: Receive 5-star review
  • +5 points: Ship orders within 24 hours

Points accumulate automatically and determine your tier level. Track progress in "Reward Status" dashboard.

Order modification depends on status:

  • Pending: Contact club directly to cancel or modify
  • Confirmed/Processing: Contact club; may be possible before shipping
  • Shipped: Cannot cancel; wait for delivery or arrange return

Use the "Contact Seller" button on order details page to communicate with clubs.
